# Product overview

North America-focused job search and application engine. Optimizes for **volume** and **time saved** while protecting application quality.

## Four layers

1. **Job discovery** — Multi-source ingestion, cleaning, dedup, freshness checks → total job pool
2. **Job decisioning** — Feed-first UX: Apply / Pass / Save / Details per card
3. **Application preparation** — Select best resume, cover letter decision, saved answers, links
4. **Submission & tracking** — Auto-submit, review-before-submit, or guided manual; log everything

## Two pools

- **Total job pool** — all live, relevant, deduplicated NA jobs
- **Auto-apply eligible pool** — subset passing automation + quality thresholds

Every job tagged: `auto-apply eligible` | `review required` | `manual only`

## Job card shows

Company, title, location, work mode, salary (if available), posting age, source, short summary, why-it-matches, automation tag

## User profile inputs

Resume versions, cover letter templates, saved answers, links (LinkedIn/GitHub/portfolio), work auth, salary pref, work mode pref, experience level, restrictions (e.g. remote only, no staffing agencies)

## Jobs page stats (top of feed)

New jobs since last login, expired since last login, saved jobs nearing deadline, jobs newly auto-apply eligible

## Initial focus

- **Industries:** tech, finance
- **Regions:** Canada, United States
- **Tech roles:** SWE, data analyst/scientist, product analyst, BA, tech support, solutions eng, QA, security analyst
- **Finance roles:** financial analyst, IB analyst, wealth mgmt, PE, corp finance, FP&A, risk, ops, compliance, credit

## Product principles

Speed with control · Volume with relevance · Automation with guardrails · Transparency in what's shown and why
